
United Airlines Accident Records: Aircraft Types and Details

Accident 1: United Airlines Flight 232
Accident ID: 1
Aircraft Type: McDonnell Douglas DC-10
Details: The flight suffered an uncontained engine failure, leading to the loss of all hydraulic systems. The crew managed to control the aircraft using differential thrust and attempted an emergency landing at Sioux Gateway Airport.

Accident 2: United Airlines Flight 93
Accident ID: 2
Aircraft Type: Boeing 757
Details: The flight was hijacked as part of the September 11 attacks. Passengers attempted to regain control of the aircraft from the hijackers.

Accident 3: United Airlines Flight 328
Accident ID: 3
Aircraft Type: Boeing 777-200
Details: Shortly after takeoff, the aircraft experienced a right engine failure. Debris from the engine fell onto residential areas below.
